Top picks (trading + automation) — sell-first mindset

1) Funding-Rate Arbitrage Bot (Institutional-friendly) — Top revenue/low time-to-prove

Pitch: Capture funding-rate divergence across CEX perpetuals — hedge spot and pocket funding payouts.
MVP features: funding-rate scanner, position simulator, auto-open hedged trades (paper mode), PnL dashboard, risk limits.
Tech: Python/Node, exchange WS/APIs, Postgres, Redis; optionally use Hummingbot patterns.
Monetization: SaaS license + profit-share. Enterprise clients pay premium.
Risks: requires capital, exchange limits, and KYC for clients. Docs & marketplaces show demand and built-in exchange guides.  ￼

⸻

2) Cross-Exchange Latency & Orderbook Arb Router (the classic money-maker)

Pitch: Auto-route execution: buy where cheapest, sell where priciest, manage transfer/hedge.
MVP features: multi-exchange orderbook cache, arb detector, simulated execution, exchange-key manager, Telegram alerts.
Tech: Go/Node for low latency, Redis orderbook cache, Postgres ledger, exchange SDKs.
Monetization: license + per-trade fee or managed service for prop funds.
Risks: latency, withdrawal times, capital fragmentation, and exchange T&Cs.

⸻

3) Smart Scanner + Auto-Exec (Grid + DCA + Rebalance) — retail pack

Pitch: Combine Grid + DCA + dynamic rebalancer tuned by volatility; clients choose risk presets.
MVP: grid strategies, DCA scheduler, backtest module, telegram alerts, simple UI.
Tech: Node + React, exchange REST/WS.
Monetization: subscription tiers (like Cryptohopper).  ￼

⸻

4) Liquidity Protection & LP Watcher (Projects + Investors)

Pitch: Detect LP withdrawals, token admin moves, rug signals — instant alerts + automated emergency actions (e.g., pause buy bots).
MVP: watch LP pairs, admin key notifications, Discord/Telegram alerts, daily contract health score.
Tech: ethers.js/web3, node workers, Postgres.
Monetization: monthly subscription + incident retainer. Whale/alerts competitors show viable pricing.  ￼

⸻

5) Whale / Mempool Sniffer + Predictive Entry Alerts

Pitch: Spot large incoming swaps or mempool flows and predict price impact; alert or execute micro-arb/limit orders.
MVP: mempool watcher, impact calc, entry suggestion, paper-exec.
Tech: mempool node (Ethereum or Solana), WebSocket, Redis.
Monetization: tiered alerts, API licensing.
Risks: noisy signals, false positives; ethical concerns around frontrunning.

⸻

6) Copy-Trading Engine with Smart Filters (influencer sell)

Pitch: Mirror vetted traders with filters (min ticket, max slippage, risk cap).
MVP: copy engine, analytics, permission system, webhook for trade events.
Tech: Node, exchange APIs, webhook infra.
Monetization: take rate on copied profits + subscription.

⸻

7) Automated Market-Making (AMM) Manager for Projects

Pitch: Auto place/tweak orders to maintain target spreads and TVL on CEX orderbooks or use LP strategies on DEXs.
MVP: place limit orders, rebalance, fee analysis, simulation.
Tech: Hummingbot-like strategies, order manager, DB.
Monetization: monthly + one-time setup.

⸻

8) Airdrop & Interaction Automation (growth/airdrop farmers)

Pitch: Automate safe protocol interactions to qualify for airdrops (bridge, stake, small txs) while tracking claimables.
MVP: task scheduler, wallet manager, script templates, reporting.
Monetization: subscription / managed service.

⸻

9) Smart Rebalancer for Treasury / DAO Automation

Pitch: Auto-maintain treasury target allocations across chains & stablecoins, with safe multisig interactions.
MVP: policy engine, simulation, multisig tx proposals (Gnosis), alerts.
Tech: backend + governance integrations.
Monetization: enterprise pricing, integration fee.

⸻

10) Social + On-chain Signal Fusion Bot (AI-assisted)

Pitch: Combine on-chain signals (wallet clusters, volume) + social signal (X/Twitter trends) to score short-term trade ideas.
MVP: data fusion engine, scoring, alerts. Nansen-like use-case is hot.  ￼
Monetization: monthly subscription; higher tiers for API access.
